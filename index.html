Bet the dice

How to play

You decide how much you want to bet by picking a value. Higher value gets harder to win!

10 euro bet - you can pick 3 numbers to compare with the dice.
50 euro bet - you pick 2 numbers.
100 euro bet -you pick 1 number.
After that you roll the dice by clicking the "click here" -button.
If the dots on the dice is the same as one of the number you picked,
you win the game and the value is yours!
If its not, you lose and just try again.
Good luck!
